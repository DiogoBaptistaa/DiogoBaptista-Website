Pre Business

My name is Diogo Baptista and I play soccer at UC Riverside. I was born in Sao paulo, Brasil and bounced around the world for the next ten years with my family. I traveled all over the world and experienced countless things. Throughout this time I was playing soccer. Playing with people from different countries was great because I got to play with people that played a different styles of soccer. After ten years of bouncing around the world, my family and I settled down in Mountain view, California. Mountain view, California was the most beautiful city I had been in so far and I couldn’t wait to live out the rest of my youth there.

I made many amazing friends that will probably be with me till the day I die,. For soccer, I played for San Jose earthquakes in the heart of San Jose, about 25 minutes from where I lived. San Jose Earthquakes was the local club so I was drawn to it because of the name being the same name as the city I was in. Every year I spent with the team I loved more than life itself. I got a lot better over the course of the next 6 years and was enjoying the sport immensely. I also started playing for a local professional soccer academy. Doing so I also made many lifelong friends that I still talk to this day.

Now, my life consists of waking up at eight in the morning, going to class, going to soccer practice from 8 to 1:30, and then going to class until 5. Although it is very tiring, I do enjoy it. Being on he UCR soccer team allowed me to learn responsibility and time management, lessons I will carry with me for the rest of my life. Along with that many amazing memories have been made that I will carry with me forever.
